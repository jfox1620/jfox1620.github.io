Hi. I'm Justin Fox.

About me: I am a data professional based in California who turns complex data into actionable insights and scalable systems. I specialize in building robust data pipelines, automating processes, and creating interactive dashboards that empower teams and organizations to make smarter, faster decisions.

Currently, I lead the Research & Evaluation (R&E) team at San Joaquin County Probation, where I design and oversee end-to-end data engineering and analytics projects. I’ve built a library of nearly 30 automated Python and SQL scripts to ingest, transform, and load multi-source data into a centralized SQL Server data warehouse, eliminating manual processes and enabling faster, more accurate reporting. I also guide the development of dynamic Power BI dashboards and comprehensive annual evaluation reports, turning raw data into clear, actionable insights for leadership and stakeholders.

Beyond my technical expertise, I have experience mentoring and leading teams, redesigning workflows, and streamlining data systems to improve efficiency and data quality. I also volunteer as a Data Strategy & Analytics Lead for Jesus Said Go, optimizing Zoho CRM and creating reports and dashboards that help project managers, donors, and staff make better decisions.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and a Microsoft Power BI Data Analyst Associate certification, n, with over a decade of experience across analytics, reporting, and data engineering. My goal is to continue growing as a data engineer and/or data scientist, tackling diverse projects, mentoring others, and delivering solutions that create real-world impact.

My specialties

This is my abridged list of skills and proficiencies.

Data Visualization

I have created many visualizations and dashboards to various stakeholders and levels of staff. I know how to choose the right visuals, how to design them for their audience, and how to emphasize what is important while preventing information overload.

Data Processing

Unlike most data analysts/scientists, I love digging through data and cleansing it. My years of fixing bad data began with Excel tricks and formulas, and evolved to using efficient SQL as well as Python.

BI/Data Engineering

I try to automate the extraction, transformation, and delivery of informative BI solutions wherever possible. The automated reports I have created for recipients minimize the risk of user error, and are built to handle unforeseen data changes.

Machine Learning

Predictive modeling through machine learning is my favorite way to bring value out of data. As a mathematician, I also love the theory and logic behind the algorithms. I have constructed both regression and classfication supervised models using real-world data.

Innovation

Whether or not it is cliché to say I am innovative, the fact remains that I find greatest motivation out of coming up my own outside-the-box ideas and working hard on them to achieve certain goals. In fact, most of the work I have done in my current role as a business analyst were originated from ideas I created.

Statistics

I am intrigued by both descriptive and inferential statistics, as well as probability. Statistical techniques are implemented in my research and analyses to help me make sense of the data at large and present the results effectively.
